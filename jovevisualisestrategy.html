Risks & Mitigation

Risk: Technical Complexity

Building a video editor (The Snipper) in the browser is hard.

Mitigation: Start with "Start/End Time" metadata markers only. Don't re-render video files; just play specific ranges.

Risk: SEO Cannibalization

Visualise pages might outrank main video pages.

Mitigation: Use rel="canonical" tags intelligently. Ensure Visualise targets "Guide" keywords, Main Site targets "Video" keywords.
